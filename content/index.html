SolarYpsi is a grass-roots effort in Ypsilanti, MI dedicated to the use of renewable energy sources and this website demonstrates our efforts in real time reporting of electrical production from solar panels. The panels are located in and around Ypsilanti and have been funded through a variety of sources. Selecting a site on the right will take you to the detail page for that site. On the map, a green marker indicates a site that has panels installed and is being monitored from this website while an orange marker is for sites that have panels installed but no monitoring. Blue markers are proposed or otherwise inactive sites.
